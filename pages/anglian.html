Welcome to our virtual event

Thank you for joining us today. Due to COVID-19 and the unprecedented times we are facing, we think it’s more important than ever to keep in touch and try new ways of engaging with you. That’s why we have developed this virtual event which contains all the information you would expect to see at an event we might hold in your village hall or community centre – but this time it’s all online! As you make your way around the room you will find information about our proposed Lincoln to Ancaster pipeline.

We recommend starting from Board 1 on the left and move around the displays – just as you might do in a real room. You can submit your comments and complete our survey at any time – just click on the link/button.
