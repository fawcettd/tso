Welcome to the Chelmsford North East Bypass and Beaulieu Station virtual engagement exhibition. The following Information presented via a virtual space is an exciting and innovative way to show case how this scheme will help Chelmsford make a stronger economic contribution to Essex as well as the East and South East of England and the UK.

We bring these boards to you during these challenges times and hope to give you the background to the project, and what environmental factors are being considered throughout the scheme. You can navigate through this virtual space in two ways.

To access using a desktop - you can opt for a guided tour by clicking on the forward and back arrows at the bottom of the screen, you will be then be taken through a guided tour in sequence. Click to interact with the content and press the Play > icon to watch a video which shows in more detail the bypass and the new Beulieu station development.

To do this on your mobile you can use the screen thumb controls to move and pan through the space. Touch to access the information.

We would be grateful if you can leave your feedback at the end. If you have any questions you would like to ask the project team, you will find contact details on the last panel or click on the QR code that will take you direct to the Chelmsford North East bypass webpage.
